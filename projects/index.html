Predicting Transplacental Drug Passage

Python • R • SQL • Scikit-learn

Built a structured dataset from scientific sources to analyze the transplacental transfer rates of drugs. Applied extensive data cleaning and preprocessing, implemented ML models including decision trees, SVMs, and neural networks, and evaluated their performance using metrics such as accuracy, ROC curves, and cross-entropy.

Deep Learning in Theoretical Chemistry

TensorFlow • PyTorch • GANs • GNNs

Used deep learning architectures like GANs and Graph Neural Networks to generate and predict chemical properties. Focused on architectural design, training stability, and interpretability in the context of molecular datasets. Employed message-passing frameworks for high-accuracy predictions.
