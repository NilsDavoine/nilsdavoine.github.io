About Me

As a passionate scientist with a background in theoretical chemistry and physics, I am dedicated to exploring and pushing the boundaries of our understanding of the natural world. My diverse skill set includes a specialization in machine learning, which allows me to approach complex problems in innovative ways.

Throughout my academic years, I have gained a broad range of knowledge and experience in the fields of chemistry and physics. I graduated from a double degree Master in bioinformatics, machine learning, and molecular dynamics. My expertise in these areas extends to a variety of subjects, including quantum chemistry, thermodynamics, and kinetics.

With a keen eye for detail and a deep passion for scientific discovery, I have worked on numerous projects in my field. These include training Neural Networks to correct and accelerate simulations, as well as utilizing computational simulations to understand self-assembled structures. If you are interested, there is more on these projects in the projects section.

As a researcher and scientist, I am constantly striving to improve and expand my knowledge and skills. I believe that scientific research and innovation have the power to create positive change in the world, and I am excited to be a part of that process. On this Github page, you will discover more about my skills, experiences, and passion for science. Join me to explore theoretical chemistry, physics, and machine learning together.

You can contact me at nils.davoine@uni.lu for more information!
